# AP2 for NSDL: Financial Potential & Revenue Model

Based on NSDL's 2024-25 statistics (₹103.22 Lakh Crore settlement value, 28,280 crore shares settled), here is a projection of the revenue potential for the Agent Payments Protocol (AP2).

## 1. Transactional Revenue (The "Mandate Fee")
Currently, delivery instructions (DIS) are often manual or semi-automated. AP2 introduces a per-instruction cryptographic mandate.

| Metric | Projection | Assumptions |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Annual Settled Instructions** | 200 Million | Based on 3.95 Cr accounts and high turnover. |
| **AP2 Platform Fee** | ₹2.00 / Mandate | Competitive with current tech processing fees. |
| **Gross Revenue (ARR)** | **₹40 Crore ($4.8M)** | Initial capture of 50% of instructional volume. |

## 2. Corporate Actions Automation (Dividends & Bonus)
NSDL facilitates massive dividend payouts. Agents using AP2 can automate the routing of these funds to specific portfolios.

| Metric | Projection | Assumptions |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Annual Dividends Processed** | ₹50,000 Crore | Conservative estimate for NSDL-held companies. |
| **Agent Routing Fee** | 0.05% | Fee for automated tax-efficient routing. |
| **Gross Revenue (ARR)** | **₹25 Crore ($3.0M)** | Capture of 5% of automated dividend flows. |

## 3. Operational Efficiency Savings (Indirect Revenue)
Automation via AP2 reduces the "Cost to Serve" for Depository Participants (DPs).

| Savings Area | Annual Savings | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Manual DIS Processing** | ₹15 Crore | Eliminating paper handling and data entry errors. |
| **Fraud Prevention** | ₹10 Crore | RSA/JWT signatures stop 99% of unauthorized transfers. |
| **Reconciliation Cost** | ₹8 Crore | Real-time settlement finality. |
| **Total Efficiency Gain** | **₹33 Crore ($4.0M)** | |

## 4. Total Financial Impact Summary

| Category | Annual Potential (INR) | Annual Potential (USD) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Direct Platform Revenue** | ₹65 Crore | $7.8 Million |
| **Indirect Operational Savings** | ₹33 Crore | $4.0 Million |
| **Network Expansion (Pledge/Loan)** | ₹20 Crore | $2.4 Million |
| **TOTAL ANNUAL VALUE** | **₹118 Crore** | **$14.2 Million** |

> [!IMPORTANT]
> This model assumes a 20-30% adoption rate within the first 24 months of NSDL allowing "Agentic Instructions". The transaction volume is the primary driver, making this a high-scalability SaaS/Protocol model.

## 5. Monthly Revenue Projections

| Scenario | Monthly Revenue (INR) | Monthly Revenue (USD) | Assumptions |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Conservative (Adoption)** | **₹10.24 Crore** | **$1.23 Million** | 20-30% volume adoption of mandates. |
| **Aggressive (Replacement)** | **₹21.70 Crore** | **$2.61 Million** | Owning the DP service layer (AMC replacement + TXN fees). |

### Monthly Breakdown (Aggressive Scenario)
- **Mandate Fees (TXN)**: ₹3.32 Crore (16.6M mandates @ ₹2)
- **Digital Custody Fee (AMC)**: ₹16.40 Crore (10% of total NSDL AMC market)
- **Corporate Action Routing**: ₹1.98 Crore

## 6. Value Proposition to NSDL
- **Zero-Trust Security**: Every instruction is cryptographically signed by the investor's agent.
- **Instant Settlement**: Reduce T+1 to Near-Real-Time.
- **Lower Compliance Risk**: Full audit trail of mandates stored on-ledger or in verifiable logs.
