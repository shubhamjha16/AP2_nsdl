## 2. Key Metrics (Aggressive Scenario)

### Agentic Commerce (General AP2)
- **Volume**: 300 million transactions/month (capturing a fraction of India's UPI/Cards/Travel volume).

### NSDL Agent (Disintermediation)
- **Volume**: 16.6 million instructions/month.
- **Client Base**: 10% of NSDL's total account market (3.95 Crore accounts) using "Agentic DPs".

## 3. The Power of "Ecosystem Synergy"
The together, these two projects move AP2 from being just a "payment app" to being the **Native Cryptographic Layer for India's Digital Economy**.
- Investors use agents for NSDL transfers (Low volume/High value).
- Shoppers use agents for e-commerce (High volume/Low value).
- **One Protocol (AP2) powers everything.**
