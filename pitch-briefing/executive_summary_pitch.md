# Executive Summary: NSDL Replacement & Revenue Model

This summary is designed for a high-level briefing (e.g., for the NSDL CISO). It explicitly maps traditional costs to new revenue streams.

## 1. WHAT IT REPLACES

| Item | Status | Replacement by AP2 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Physical/Electronic DIS** | **REPLACED** | **Securities Instruction Mandates**: Cryptographically signed, instant, and 100% auditable. |
| **DP Portal Logins** | **REPLACED** | **Agentic Handshake**: Zero-trust authentication via the investor's private key. No passwords to phish. |
| **Manual Verification** | **REPLACED** | **Automated Handoff**: The protocol verifies the "Chain of Trust" (Investor -> DP -> NSDL) in milliseconds. |
| **Broker Service Layer** | **BYPASSED** | **Direct-to-NSDL Flow**: Agents act as "Micro-DPs," removing the need for traditional intermediary service teams. |

## 2. MONTHLY REVENUE (THE REPLACEMENT MODEL)

If we replace the traditional DP service layer with an AP2-powered Digital Custody model:

| Revenue Stream | Monthly Potential (INR) | Calculation Base |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Instruction Mandate Fees** | **₹3.32 Crore** | 1.66 crore instructions/month @ ₹2.00 each. |

| **Automated Div. Routing** | **₹1.98 Crore** | 0.05% fee on ₹4,000 Crore monthly automated dividends. |
| **TOTAL REVENUE** | **₹21.70 Crore** | **$2.61 Million USD / Month** |

## 3. WHY THE CISO SHOULD CARE
- **Eliminates Ledger Fraud**: Since every instruction is signed by the user's hardware-bound key, DPs cannot "fake" or "move" shares internally.
- **T+0 Settlement Ready**: By removing manual verification, NSDL can move to near-instant settlement safely.
- **Regulatory Proof**: Provides a perfect, unbreakable audit trail for SEBI compliance.
